Mental Arithmetic

Instructions


This is a mental arithmetic task.

Your goal is to correctly answer as many of the mental math questions as possible. There are six blocks of questions and you have one minute to answer as many within each block.

Input your answer using the keyboard and when you have entered the number you think is correct hit enter. Within each the questions go from very easy to very tricky as you progress.
Press the right arrow key on your keyboard to start the task.
